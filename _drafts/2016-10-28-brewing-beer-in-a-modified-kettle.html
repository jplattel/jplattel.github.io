I finally got myself a proper kettle for brewing beer. It's a modified Weck kettle that is normally used for the wecking process.

The idea started last year when I started brewing with Martijn from Bentrotsch.

Future modifications might be:

  • A more sturdy plumbing system, copper, to retain heat and make it more easy to isolate, instead of the cheap plastic. Also required for the next item on this list:
  • A pump for circulating the wort, this should also make filtering easier.
  • A PID controller for temperature control. Since the thermostat that build into the kettle can vary more than 10 degrees Celcius!
