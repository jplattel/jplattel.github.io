QS Amsterdam Recap #1

27-09-2010
Amsterdam Personal Informatics , photos , QS , Quantified Self , self-tracking

On 20 september the first Quantified Self Amsterdam Meetup took place in het volkskrantgebouw. More then 60 people attended and we had guests from Germany and France! A full recap can be found on the main QS blog and the video’s on vimeo. Sebastiaan ter Burg provided the video/photo capture and Kees Plattel made a wonderful intro!

I really enjoyed this amazing meetup with interesting talks! If you are interested sign up for our meetup group and we will let you know when the next event is! Or do you have a personal story to share, feel free to contact me!
